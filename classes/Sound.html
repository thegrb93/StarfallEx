Methods

 Sound:isPlaying ()
Returns whether the sound is being played.
 Sound:play ()
Starts to play the sound.
 Sound:setPitch (pitch, fade)
Sets the pitch of the sound.

Parameters:

  • pitch: Pitch to set to, between 0 and 255.
  • fade: Time in seconds to transition to this new pitch.
 Sound:setSoundLevel (level)
Sets the sound level in dB.

Parameters:

  • level: dB level, see Vale Dev Wiki, for information on the value to use.
 Sound:setVolume (vol, fade)
Sets the volume of the sound.

Parameters:

  • vol: Volume to set to, between 0 and 1.
  • fade: Time in seconds to transition to this new volume.
 Sound:stop (fade)
Stops the sound from being played.

Parameters:

  • fade: Time in seconds to fade out, if nil or 0 the sound stops instantly.
